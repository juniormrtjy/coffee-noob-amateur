coffee benefits

1. Keeps heart problems away

A survey of 140,000 people by the School of Public Health at Harvard University (USA) showed that four daily doses of coffee reduce by up to 11% the risk of heart failure, which is a condition in which the heart cannot pump blood properly for the body. Apparently, those responsible for this benefit are the drink's polyphenols. "They fight free radicals in the body that altered the DNA and increase bad cholesterol in the body, causing heart problems such as heart attacks, for example", explains Maria Fernanda Vischi D'Ottavio, nutritionist at HCor.
